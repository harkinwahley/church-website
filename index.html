About Our Ministry

The Way of God Bible Ministry, also known as Mountain of Praise, was founded on the divine mandate to spread the unadulterated Word of God and raise disciples for Christ. For nearly two decades, we have been a beacon of hope and transformation in our community, fostering spiritual growth, nurturing families, and impacting lives through the power of God's love.

Our journey has been one of faith, dedication, and countless testimonies. As we approach our 20th Anniversary in December, we look back with gratitude and look forward with renewed zeal to continue fulfilling our mission of making a way in the wilderness and rivers in the desert, just as promised in Isaiah 43:19.

Join us as we walk in the path of righteousness, experiencing God's miraculous hand in every aspect of our lives.

Our Ministries

Youth Ministry - "Generation of Fire"

Our Youth Ministry, "Generation of Fire," is dedicated to empowering young people to live vibrant, purpose-driven lives rooted in Christ. We provide a dynamic environment for spiritual growth, leadership development, mentorship, and fellowship through weekly Bible studies, engaging workshops, fun social events, and community outreach.

We believe in raising a generation that will stand strong in faith, influence their peers positively, and be agents of change in their world for God's glory. Join us every Saturday for our exciting youth fellowship!

Learn More

Children's Ministry - "God's Little Lambs"

The Children's Ministry, "God's Little Lambs," is committed to nurturing our youngest members in a safe, fun, and Christ-centered environment. We teach biblical truths through engaging lessons, creative activities, songs, and age-appropriate discussions, helping children build a strong foundation in faith from an early age.

Our dedicated teachers ensure every child feels loved, valued, and excited about learning God's Word. We prepare them to understand Christian values, preparing them for future exams and life challenges.
